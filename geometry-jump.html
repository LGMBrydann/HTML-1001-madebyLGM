Geometry Jump is a challenging skill game in which you have to dash through a series of high-speed platform mazes. Leap through the neon levels and dodge the spikes, barriers, and other traps with your little geometric cube. Don’t be fooled: this one-button control game is fiendishly difficult!

How do you play Geometry Jump?

In this jumping game, your cube will speed forward automatically. It’s up to you to time your jumps perfectly and guide it across the floating platforms, up the stairs, and past the deadly spikes. If your cube smashes into any obstacles it’ll be blown apart.

This game contains jumping levels and flying levels. Collect the stars and search for the magic portal that marks the end of each section, and also acts as a checkpoint. The progress bar at the top of the screen will show you how far you still have to go to complete the level.

If your cube gets smashed or falls into the abyss, it’ll respawn at the last portal you passed through and you can continue playing from there. Can you dash through all levels of Geometry Jump?

How do you change the game speed in Geometry Jump?

If you’re struggling with a particular level, or the game feels a little impossible, don’t have a meltdown. You can go to the game options menu and lower the speed. Tap the pause button in the top right corner of the screen. A pop-up menu will appear. The bottom slider will allow you to change the pace of the game. Slow the game down to make it easier, or increase the speed to play Geometry Jump at its hardest.

How do you unlock custom cubes and jets in Geometry Jump?

You can unlock a range of new cubes, jets, and other geometric designs in the Shop menu. Tap on the pause button and select the blue menu button located at the bottom of the pop-up menu (next to the purple PLAY-button). This will take you to the main menu. Tap on the shopping cart to view the Shop.
